Eagle Exchange

A platform for BC students to buy, sell, and exchange goods

Anran Du
Created in ISYS 2160 Intro to Programming using Swift for iOS App Development taught by Professor John Gallaugher
Journal Evalation

In this iOS app, students are able to buy, sell, and exchange their personal items such as event tickets and textbooks. Storing all of the information on Google Firebase, unlike traditional goods-exchanging platforms scuh as Facebook, the app gives students a designated place to exchange their personal goods.
